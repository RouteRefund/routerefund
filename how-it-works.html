How RouteRefund works

Book normally. Let RouteRefund watch after.

RouteRefund helps travelers capture eligible savings after they already bought a flight.

1

Create account

Set up a private RouteRefund account so your trips stay tied to you.

2

Forward confirmation

Send your airline booking email to trips@routerefund.com or enter the basics manually.

3

We monitor

RouteRefund watches for eligible same-passenger, acceptable-cabin fare opportunities.

4

You get savings

If savings are captured, you receive the refund or flight credit.

What happens when a lower fare appears?

RouteRefund reviews the fare rules, passenger match, cabin, timing, and itinerary requirements. If it looks eligible, the owner team can move quickly under the authorization provided when the trip is added.
