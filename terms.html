Terms of Service

This page is a working draft and should be reviewed by counsel before public customer launch.

Service

RouteRefund monitors eligible booked flights and may assist with rebooking, cancellation, refund, or flight-credit opportunities.

Customer authorization

By adding a trip, the customer authorizes RouteRefund to review and pursue eligible savings within the stated trip terms.

Limitations

Airline rules, fare availability, refund eligibility, and credit rules may change. RouteRefund cannot guarantee savings.

Payment

RouteRefund may receive compensation from savings captured. Details should be disclosed at the appropriate payment step.
