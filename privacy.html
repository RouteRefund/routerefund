Privacy Policy

This page is a working draft and should be reviewed by counsel before public customer launch.

Information we collect

Account information, trip details, passenger details needed to identify bookings, forwarded confirmation content, support messages, and operational notes.

How we use information

To create accounts, monitor trips, verify eligible savings, communicate with customers, support operations, and protect RouteRefund.

What we do not collect in trip forms

RouteRefund trip forms should not collect raw credit card numbers.

Questions can be sent to support@routerefund.com.
