Privacy

Privacy Policy

RouteRefund collects only the account and trip information needed to monitor eligible savings and support customers.

Information we collect

Account details, customer acknowledgements, passenger names, date of birth when needed for trip lookup, airline, record locator, route/date, amount paid, forwarded confirmation email content, support messages, and operational owner notes.

How we use information

To create accounts, verify bookings, parse forwarded confirmations, monitor fare changes, contact customers about savings, maintain internal review records, and protect RouteRefund from misuse.

Access controls

Customer trip records are protected by account login and database row-level security. Owner-only notes, monitoring checks, and forwarded confirmation review data are restricted to approved RouteRefund owner accounts.

Payment information

RouteRefund trip forms do not ask for raw card numbers. If billing is added, payment should be handled by a secure hosted provider such as Stripe.

Contact

Questions can be sent to support@routerefund.com.
