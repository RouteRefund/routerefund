Trust & safety

Your travel details are sensitive. We treat them that way.

RouteRefund is being built with customer privacy, secure access, and minimal data collection as core requirements.

We do not store card numbers

Trip forms never ask for raw card numbers. Payments should happen through secure Stripe links.

Customer-only dashboards

Customers should only see their own trips through Supabase row-level security.

Restricted owner access

Owner tools are separate and intended only for approved RouteRefund partners.

Data we may need

Passenger name, DOB, confirmation code, airline, route/date from forwarded emails, price paid, and operational notes. We collect only what helps us identify and monitor the trip.
