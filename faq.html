RouteRefund FAQ

What does RouteRefund do?

You book the flight. We make sure you get the cheapest rate by watching for eligible fare drops after purchase.

How do I add a trip?

Forward your confirmation email to trips@routerefund.com or enter the trip manually in your dashboard.

When does RouteRefund get paid?

RouteRefund is paid from savings we help capture. Exact payment details are handled during the savings/payment step.

Do you need my credit card?

No card numbers should be entered into trip forms. Payment should use secure Stripe-hosted links.

What if there are no savings?

Then there is nothing to capture. RouteRefund keeps watching while the trip remains eligible.
