Getting started

What does RouteRefund do?

We watch booked flights for eligible savings and bring you reviewed next steps.

How do I add a trip?

Forward the airline confirmation to trips@routerefund.com or enter the basics in your dashboard.

Which trips work best?

Direct-booked U.S. airline flights with a confirmation code and enough time before departure.

Can restrictive fares work?

Sometimes. We review rules before presenting any savings path.

Safety

Do you need my airline password?

No. Never send passwords, one-time codes, or email access.

Will you cancel or rebook without me?

No. You approve before any airline action.

Do you store card numbers?

No. Payment, when needed, belongs in secure hosted checkout.

Savings

Is savings guaranteed?

No. Prices and airline rules change.

When does RouteRefund get paid?

Only from eligible savings we help capture, with payment details shown before billing.

Cash or credit?

Airline rules decide. We label credits and tradeoffs clearly before action.
