Simple customer flow

Add the trip once. We handle the watching.

RouteRefund is built for travelers who booked directly with the airline and do not want to keep refreshing prices. Submit the basics or forward the confirmation email, then use your dashboard to track status, delete test trips, or see when a trip enters review.

Manual trip entry

Choose airline, enter confirmation code, passenger name, DOB, and authorization. For supported airlines, lookup starts automatically.

Forward confirmation

Send the airline confirmation to trips@routerefund.com. RouteRefund can organize details from the email without inbox access.
