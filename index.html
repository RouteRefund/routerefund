FAQ

How does RouteRefund get paid?

RouteRefund is paid from savings we help capture. Details are presented during the savings/payment step.

Do I need to approve every fare change?

When you add a trip, you authorize RouteRefund to pursue eligible savings within your selected trip terms, such as same passenger and acceptable cabin/itinerary requirements.
