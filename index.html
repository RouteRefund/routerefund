Flight price drop help after you book

Booked a flight? RouteRefund watches for savings.

Create an account, add your confirmed trip, and we monitor for lower fares that may qualify for refund, credit, or rebooking opportunities.

Create accountLog in

We never change or cancel your trip without your approval.

What this offers

Price-drop monitoring for real trips

  • Save booked flights in your private account
  • Include flight number, route, date, price paid, and confirmation number
  • Get notified if a meaningful price drop is found
  • Approve next steps before RouteRefund helps with refund/rebooking options
  • Commission-based model only when savings are actually captured

How it works

1. Make an account

Sign up with your name, email, and password.

2. Add your booked flight

Enter flight details and confirmation number so the trip can be verified.

3. We monitor and alert you

If a lower fare appears, you approve what happens next.

Security and trust

No card required at signup

We are not collecting payment info in this MVP. If payments are added later, use Stripe Checkout only.

Private customer accounts

Trip data is stored per signed-in user. Customers only see their own trips.

Owner access is separate

The owner dashboard is not linked from the customer site and should not be public until role-based access is finished.
