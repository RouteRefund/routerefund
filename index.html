What RouteRefund does

  • Stores your booked trip details in your account
  • Watches for fare-drop and credit opportunities
  • Uses your trip authorization to protect speed when prices move
  • Focuses on same passenger, same cabin, same or acceptable itinerary changes
  • Only gets paid from savings we help capture

Security-first by design

Flight details and passenger info are sensitive. RouteRefund should collect only what is needed, keep owner access restricted, and use Stripe-hosted payment flows instead of storing card numbers.

  • No credit card numbers entered into RouteRefund forms
  • Payments should be handled through Stripe Checkout
  • Customers only see their own trips
  • Owner dashboard access is separated and restricted in Supabase
  • Trip authorization is captured before monitoring starts
