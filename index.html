Customer safety answers

No airline password

MVP starts with booking details only. If deeper access is ever needed, it should use secure OAuth, not password sharing.

Stripe for payments

Cards are handled by Stripe Checkout or Payment Links. RouteRefund should not store raw card data.

You stay in control

No cancel, rebook, refund claim, or fee charge happens without clear customer approval.
