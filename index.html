FAQ

How does RouteRefund get paid?

RouteRefund is paid from savings we help capture. Details are presented during the savings/payment step through secure payment links.

Will RouteRefund cancel or rebook my flight?

No automatic airline changes happen. If a reviewed opportunity looks eligible, RouteRefund contacts you with the next safe step before any booking, cancellation, or rebooking action.

Do I need to approve every fare change?

When you add a trip, you authorize RouteRefund to monitor eligible opportunities within your selected trip terms. RouteRefund reviews passenger, cabin, itinerary, and rule match before customer follow-up.
