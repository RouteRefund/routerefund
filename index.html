Simple customer flow

No “free work” language. The promise is clean: monitoring is free; RouteRefund earns a success fee from confirmed savings.

1. Submit trip

Customer adds flight number, travel date, booking price, and email. No airline password needed for the demo MVP.

2. We watch fares

Background software checks prices on a schedule. Tomo reviews flagged drops and policy risk.

3. Customer saves

If the customer gets a refund, credit, or cheaper rebook, Stripe collects the agreed commission.

Trust & safety answers

Copy that makes customers feel secure before entering flight and payment details.

Do you need my airline password?

No for the MVP. Customers submit booking details and receive guided instructions. If deeper airline access is ever needed, it should use official authorization only.

How is payment handled?

RouteRefund uses Stripe Checkout/Payment Links. Card data never touches our servers.

Can RouteRefund change my flight without permission?

No. Any cancellation, rebooking, refund request, or payment requires customer approval.

What do I agree to?

Customers accept clear terms: monitoring authorization, success-fee percentage, no guarantee of savings, and approval required before account-impacting actions.
