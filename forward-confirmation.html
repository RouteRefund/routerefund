Fastest setup

Forward your flight confirmation.

The easiest way to add a trip is to forward the original airline confirmation email.

trips@routerefund.com

What to forward

Airline booking confirmations, receipt emails, itinerary emails, and rebooking confirmations.

What we look for

Passenger name, route, dates, confirmation code, cabin, and amount paid.

What happens next

RouteRefund reviews the email, creates or updates the trip, and marks it as watching.

Can’t find the email?

Use manual entry instead. You only need passenger details, confirmation code, airline, and price paid.
