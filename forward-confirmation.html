Fastest setup

Forward your flight confirmation.

The easiest way to add a trip is to forward the original airline confirmation email. RouteRefund uses the booking details to prepare monitoring while you keep control of the reservation.

trips@routerefund.com

Forward from the email address you want tied to your RouteRefund account when possible.

Keep it safe

  • Do not include airline passwords or login codes
  • Do not send payment card numbers
  • Do not forward unrelated personal documents
  • RouteRefund will ask before any airline action
1

Forward the original email

Airline booking confirmations, receipts, itinerary emails, and rebooking confirmations are the best sources.

2

We extract the trip basics

Passenger name, route, dates, confirmation code, cabin, and amount paid are reviewed for completeness.

3

Monitoring starts conservatively

RouteRefund creates or updates the trip, keeps unverified savings language general, and follows up if anything is missing.

Helpful to include

  • The full airline email thread if the trip was changed
  • Seat/cabin or schedule constraints we should preserve
  • Whether you prefer cash refund or airline credit if both are available

Not needed

  • Airline account passwords or one-time codes
  • Email inbox access or screenshots containing unrelated mail
  • Payment card numbers, CVV, or billing account credentials
