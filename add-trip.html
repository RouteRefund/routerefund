What you need

Keep setup simple and privacy-safe.

RouteRefund can begin with the same facts travelers already see in a normal airline confirmation. If a detail is missing, the trip stays in intake review instead of asking for sensitive account access.

Have readyAirline, confirmation code, passenger name, travel date, route, cabin, and the amount paid if shown.
Leave outAirline passwords, one-time codes, inbox access, payment card numbers, passport images, and security answers.
After submissionYour dashboard shows intake or monitoring status before any reviewed savings next step is shared.

What happens after login?

Your dashboard gives you two choices: forward your flight confirmation to trips@routerefund.com, or manually enter the basic trip information. RouteRefund reviews matches before contacting you, and will not book, cancel, or rebook airline travel unless you separately authorize that step.

Privacy reminderRouteRefund never needs airline passwords, inbox access, one-time codes, card numbers, passport images, or security answers to begin monitoring.
